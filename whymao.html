Ko Tarore nō Aotearoa he tangata pakihi rongonui nā te āheinga o te whakauru i te auahatanga, te manawanui, me te mātaki i ngā mea katoa e pā ana ki āna mahi. E kākahu ana ia i te wairua pakihi kaha, me te rapu i ngā huarahi auaha hei whakaoti i ngā wero me te whakapai ake i te ao o te hāpori. Nā tōna āheinga rangahau, i kitea e ia ngā whai wāhitanga i ngā wā e kitehia ana e te tangata te toenga o ngā raru, e kākahu ana ia i ngā pūmanawa hei rongoā raru. Ahakoa ko te whai wāhitanga i te hapori, te whakatairanga i ngā whakaaro hou, me te waihanga panoni roa, ko te whakaaro pakihi a Tarore me tōna ārahi he tino tohu i ngā mahi katoa e toro atu ana ia ki reira. Ko tōna āhua pakihi he tohu nō tāna hiahia i te angitu whaiaro, me te ū ki te pātea i te hāpori.
